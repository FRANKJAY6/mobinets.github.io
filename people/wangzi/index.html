Biography

Zi Wang received his B.S. degree at the School of Computer Science and Engineering in University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). He is currently a M.S. student in UESTC. His research interests focus on Mobile Edge Networks. He is also interested in ad hoc networks and innovative mobile Apps.
His co-advisors are Prof. Geyong Min and A.P. Zhiwei Zhao.
Our research group can be found here.
